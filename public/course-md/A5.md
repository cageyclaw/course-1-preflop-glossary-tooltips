# Play Against Opens

## Objective
Build a clear response tree versus opens{:Open}: **3‑bet{:3-bet}, call{:Call}, or fold{:Fold}** based on position{:Position} and opener tendencies.

## Definitions
- **Defend{:Defend}:** Continue versus an open{:Open} (call{:Call} or 3‑bet{:3-bet}).
- **Squeeze{:Squeeze}:** 3‑bet{:3-bet} after an open{:Open} and one or more callers.
- **Isolation:** Raising{:Raise} to play heads‑up{:Heads-Up} versus a weaker opener.

## Rules
1. **Defend{:Defend} widest in the BB{:BB}.** You already have money in and close action.
2. **Call{:Call} more IP{:IP}, 3‑bet{:3-bet} more OOP{:OOP}.** Position{:Position} dictates leverage.
3. **Avoid cold‑calling too wide OOP{:OOP}.** That’s a leak factory.
4. **Squeeze{:Squeeze} bigger** when there are callers in between.

![Squeeze risk lane](/course-md/squeeze-risk-lane.svg)

## Exceptions
- **Weak openers:** widen value 3‑bets and isolate larger.
- **Multiway{:Multiway} pots:** tighten speculative calls{:Call} OOP{:OOP}.
- **High rake{:Rake} / low edges:** favor 3‑bet{:3-bet} or fold{:Fold} rather than flatting.
