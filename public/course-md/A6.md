# Short Stack Play

## Objective
Adjust your ranges{:Range} and sizing when stacks are **40bb or less** so you stop bleeding in shallow{:Shallow Stacks}‑stack spots.

## Definitions
- **Effective stack{:Effective Stack}:** The smallest stack involved in the hand.
- **Jam:** All‑in preflop shove.
- **Equity realization{:Equity Realization}:** How well a hand converts equity{:Equity} into value postflop.

## Rules
1. **Open{:Open} smaller.** 2.0–2.2x is standard when shallow{:Shallow Stacks}.
2. **Prioritize high‑card value.** Trash suited connectors{:Suited Connectors} lose power.
3. **3‑bet{:3-bet} jam more linearly.** Shallow stacks{:Shallow Stacks} reduce postflop skill edge.
4. **Avoid speculative flats.** Low SPR{:SPR} removes implied odds{:Implied Odds}.

![Stack depth scale](/course-md/stack-depth-scale.svg)

## Exceptions
- **Loose{:Loose} callers:** widen value opens{:Open} and punish with bigger sizes.
- **Nitty tables:** steal more from late position{:Position}.
- **ICM pressure:** tighten all‑in bluffs, especially as a mid stack.
